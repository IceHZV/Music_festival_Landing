ABOUTS US

DJI Festival is an international hub for music fans from all walks of life, regardless of their racial, cultural, gender and social diversities, uniting them DJI the festival values which they all commonly share.
The festival was set up with an intention to promote the truly special, unique, playful and aesthetic aspects of the underground movement both locally and internationally, showcasing newcomers and legends of the music scene, inviting the convergence of art and music between generations.
